MY MEMORIES IN MT . SANBANG

When you are driving close to the arrival of the mountain, you can already take a rough view of it. When you arrive, there is a parking lot provided thankfully. So my family and I parked our car and we enjoyed a pleasant walk up the steps around the lower temple. The traditional and sophisticated architecture of the temples and the calming environment was stunning.

We were planning to walk along the Yongmeori Coast, so we bought the entrance tickets sold for both. The steps to the top cave temple was very steep and it was a hard time, but the scenery of the sea as hiking was breath-taking. When we reached the cave, we met the Golden Buddha and we made offerings.

As we come down the mountain, it was nice to visit souvenir stores which sell a variety of iconic Jeju stone figures and more.
