Ramada is a hotel chain brand owned by Wyndham Hotel Group that operates over 7,200 hotels worldwide. The company is based in New Jersey and operates reservation networks that cover the world. ‘Ramada’ in Spanish term means "shady resting place".

Ramada Plaza Jeju was the first ocean view hotel in South Korea and is Located only five minutes away from Jeju International Airport and Jeju Port. Ramada Plaza Jeju building is surrounded by the emerald ocean and gives you a unique experience as if you are in a cruise ship on the ocean all year round.

The hotel with unique, prestige interior designs has various types of rooms and suites, and banquet halls in different sizes including Grand Ballroom capable of accommodating up to 1,500 guests for seminars, meetings, and wedding ceremonies. With other convenient and versatile facilities such as Fitness health club, spa, pool, bar and restaurants, coffee shops, gift shop, karaoke, Ramada Plaza Jeju provides guests with some of the best hotel services and experience.
