MY MEMORIES IN YONGNUNI OREUM

This is one of the most awesome hikes in Jeju. I recommend hiking the whole circle. The scenery of windmills and farm is breathtaking!

First my family and I drove to the entrance of Yongnuni Oreum and parked our car at the provided parking lot. It was very convenient that they had it!

You can buy a ticket for a 'rail bike', which takes you a tour through and around the mountain on a rail. However, including me, people tend to take a hike because the way up to the mountain, you can meet horses grown in Jeju. Also, although the slopes make you sweat a lot the cool wind dries it off.

When you reach the very top, the horizontal landscape with gental curved mountains, the windmills, the green and brown nature really represent Jeju.
